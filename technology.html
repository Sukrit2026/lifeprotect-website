Technology & Innovation

Pioneering next-generation antimicrobial protection with SpectraShield™ and Fosshield® fiber technology.

Core Technology

SpectraShield™ Technology

The SpectraShield™ GUARDIAN

The SpectraShield™ GUARDIAN is the first-ever REUSABLE antimicrobial-protected N95 respirator mask. FDA-cleared for 8-hour extended use with continued evaluation for additional extended-use applications, it is engineered with next-generation microbe-fighting technology and designed to be reused for up to 28 days. Made in the USA with domestically sourced materials.

Fosshield® Fiber Technology

Raw materials under exclusive license made in New Hampshire, USA. Fosshield® fiber is the first and only antimicrobial fiber successfully embedded with a natural balance of silver and copper. It is the first to ever receive FDA and EU (CE Mark) clearance for use in a medical device.

How It Works

Its proprietary, safe, natural, and effective Fosshield® antimicrobial technology penetrates the microbe's cell wall, attacks its metabolism, and disrupts reproduction. The patented technology, which embeds a non-toxic silver and copper zeolite into a fabric's fibers, ensures protection for individuals from infectious disease but also for mitigating the spread of disease.
