Our Companies

LifeProtect operates through two specialized subsidiaries — Nexera Medical and MidAmerica Safety Corp — combining breakthrough antimicrobial technology with scalable American manufacturing.

Subsidiary

Nexera Medical

Nexera Medical is an innovator in infection-fighting respiratory protection. Our flagship SpectraShield antimicrobial respirators combine NIOSH-certified filtration with embedded copper-silver zeolite technology to kill 99% of bacteria and viruses. FDA-cleared for extended multi-patient use and Health Canada and EU cleared for reusable use, our masks help reduce waste and cost while improving safety.

  • 3 FDA 510(k) clearances
  • N95 & N99 certified
  • Health Canada 28-day reusability approved
  • Made with U.S. materials and Fosshield technology

Subsidiary

MidAmerica Safety Corp

MidAmerica Safety Corp is a U.S.-based manufacturer of FDA-approved Level 3 surgical masks and NIOSH-certified N95 and N99 respirators. Located in Elk Grove Village, IL, we maintain scalable production capacity and a secure, domestic supply chain to support emergency and routine healthcare PPE needs. MASC proudly serves as OEM for Nexera SpectraShield respirator technology.

  • Fully operational U.S. manufacturing lines
  • Immediate surge readiness
  • Products listed on Federal Schedules (FSS, DLA, VA MSPV)
