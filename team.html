Leadership

The People Behind LifeProtect

Our leadership team brings together deep experience in healthcare, public health policy, government logistics, med tech, and private equity. Our leaders guide the company’s mission to develop innovative PPE solutions while strengthening America’s domestic manufacturing base.

Mavara Mirza-Agrawal, MD

President & CEO

Dr. Mavara Mirza-Agrawal is a board-certified internist and pediatrician dedicated to public health and pandemic preparedness. As CEO of LifeProtect, she brings clinical leadership and policy expertise to PPE innovation. She also serves as Co-Chair of the Florida AAP Disaster Preparedness Executive Committee and represents the AAP on the AMCHP Emergency Preparedness Committee.

Akhil K. Agrawal

Co-Founder & Board Chairman

Akhil Agrawal is the Managing Partner of Litera Capital and a seasoned investor. He co-founded AMD in 1993 and served as its President until 2020, transforming the firm from a healthcare distributor into a resilient supply chain and logistics leader supporting health systems and governmental entities. As co-founder of LifeProtect, he has played a pivotal role in the development of the company’s innovation and strategic direction.

Greg Burel

Chief Strategy Officer

Mr. Burel served as the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), Office of the Assistant Secretary for Preparedness and Response (ASPR) Director of the Strategic National Stockpile (SNS) from March 2007 to January 2020. In this role, he directed the nation’s premier medical materiel preparedness and response organization.

Jason L. Radick, MD

Chief Medical Officer

Dr. Radick is a physician whose nearly 30-year career spans patient care, leadership, medical education, and industry collaboration. He currently serves as Chief Medical Officer for LifeProtect, an innovation-focused domestic manufacturer of respiratory protection. He previously served as Chief Medical Officer for American Medical Depot, where he led the Acute Care business line and emergency preparedness efforts across local, state, and federal agencies and departments. Dr. Radick is a recognized national speaker and key opinion leader for pharmaceutical and medical device companies. He earned his Doctor of Medicine from the University of Miami Miller School of Medicine and a Bachelor of Arts in Biology from Washington University in St. Louis.

John D. Zarkowsky

Chief Innovation Officer

Mr. Zarkowsky has led enterprise-level initiatives that have shaped future medical capabilities, defined operational knowledge, skills, and abilities, and aligned policy, resources, and execution across large, complex organizations. His work in Navy Medicine consistently bridges policy, finance, and operations to deliver sustainable readiness outcomes. He is a Senior Executive Fellow of the Harvard Kennedy School and a recipient of the Legion of Merit and Distinguished Civil Service Award.
